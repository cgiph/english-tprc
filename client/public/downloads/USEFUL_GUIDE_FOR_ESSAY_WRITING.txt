PTE ESSAY GUIDE

1. INTRODUCTION (2-4 simple sentences)

Pattern: state the topic + give a simple opinion + add contrasting ideas

Template:

    -   The topic is about (main idea).
    -   This is an important issue today since (reason why).
    -   I think (your simple opinion with a simple reason).
    -   However, some people think that (add a contrasting idea).

Example topic: “Is online learning good or bad?”

-   The topic is about online learning. This is important today since many students study on
    the internet. I think online learning is good because it is easy and saves time.
    However, some people think that online learning is bad because students cannot meet
    friends.


2. BODY PARAGRAPH 2 (3-4 simple sentences)

Pattern: give one reason + example

Template:

    -   In my opinion, (go back to your simple opinion)
    -   One reason is that (main reason)
    -   For example, (very simple example – should be connected to the main reason)
    -   This shows that (link back to the topic)
    -   Another point is (extra small idea – connected to your main reason and simple
        example)

Example:

    -   In my opinion, online learning is good. One reason is that it saves time. For example,
        students can study at home and do not travel to school. This shows that online
        learning is helpful for busy people. Another point is that it can save money because
        students do not pay for bus or food.

3. BODY PARAGRAPH 3 (3-4 simple sentences)

Pattern: start with a contrasting + reason + example

Template:

   -   On the other hand, (go back to the contrasting idea from your introduction)
   -   This is because (reason from your contrasting idea)
   -   For example, (very simple example – should be connected to the contrasting idea)
   -   This is why (solution to the issue presented from your example)

Example:

   -   On the other hand, some people think online learning is bad. This is because
       students cannot meet friends. For example, they stay at home and feel lonely. This
       is why some people say students should go to school sometimes.



4. CONCLUSION (3-4 sentences)

Pattern: restate opinion + close simply

Template:

   -   In conclusion, (repeat your opinion in a different way)
   -   There are many good reasons for this.
   -   Although (repeat the contrasting idea in a different way)
   -   I believe that (what you think will happen in the future – supporting more points from
       your good idea + add reasons and examples – from the 2nd paragraph)

Example:

   -   In conclusion, online learning is good for many students. There are many good
       reasons for this. Although some people think it is bad for social life, I believe that
       more people will use online learning in the future because it saves time and money.

THE COMPLETE ESSAY

The topic is about online learning. This is important today since many students study on
the internet. I think online learning is good because it is easy and saves time.
However, some people think that online learning is bad because students cannot meet
friends.



In my opinion, online learning is good. One reason is that it saves time. For example,
students can study at home and do not travel to school. This shows that online learning is
helpful for busy people. Another point is that it can save money because students do not
pay for bus or food.



On the other hand, some people think online learning is bad. This is because students
cannot meet friends. For example, they stay at home and feel lonely. This is why some
people say students should go to school sometimes.



In conclusion, online learning is good for many students. There are many good reasons for
this. Although some people think it is bad for social life, I believe that more people will use
online learning in the future because it saves time and money.
